The Author

Vivian Pontes

Writer · Illustrator · World-Builder

Vivian Pontes writes dark fantasy with her heart in her hand and characters who bleed out loud. Based in Portugal, she believes that books are portals — and that every story changes the world of the one who reads it.

The Sacrifice of Eternis saga is her declaration of this belief: a universe built on moral ambiguity, ancient magic, and the kind of emotional devastation that stays with you long after the last page.

She is also a digital illustrator — and the covers of the saga were born as art before they existed as words.

"My work is not for those seeking comfort. It is for those who want to feel something real — even if it breaks them."
